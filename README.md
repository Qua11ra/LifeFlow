# **LifeFlow**
It's my best pet-project, includes: frontend, backend, infra, mobile and desktop apps. I'll finish it maybe)
## Idea
It's the app, that much more than a to-do list.
### *"Dream"*
You can add goals you want to do - *"Dreams"*. "*Dream*" is the biggest goal of your life.
### *"Step"*
*"Dream"* is quite big, so you need to split by *"Steps"*. When you complete one *"Step"*, you get nearer to your *"Dream"*. It includes *"Tasks"* and *"Notes"*.
### *"Task"*
Simple thing. You complete the *"Tasks"* to finish the *"step"*.
### *"Note"*
Simple thing too for remember something. You can add *"Notes* into the *"Dreams"*, *"Steps"* and *"Tasks"*.
### Extra
Add, update, delete and check your *"Dreams"*, *"Steps"* and *"Tasks"*.
